VENUS FACTS

  • Second planet from the Sun
  • Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Day length: 243 Earth days
  • Year length: 225 Earth days
  • Atmosphere: Mainly carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid
  • Surface features: Volcanic plains and mountains; extremely high surface pressure.

VENUS MISSION

Magellan

Mapped the surface of Venus using radar from 1989 to 1994.

Magellan provided detailed maps of Venus, revealing its geological features and volcanic activity.

Venera 7

The first spacecraft to successfully land on Venus in 1970.

Venera 7 transmitted data back to Earth from the harsh surface of Venus.

Pioneer Venus

Conducted a detailed study of Venus in the late 1970s and early 1980s.

Pioneer Venus revealed much about Venus's atmosphere and surface conditions.

Venus Express

A European Space Agency mission that studied Venus from 2006 to 2014.

Venus Express provided insights into the planet's atmosphere, climate, and surface processes.
