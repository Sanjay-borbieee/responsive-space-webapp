MARS FACTS

  • Fourth planet from the Sun
  • Diameter: 6,779 km (0.53 x Earth)
  • Day length: 24 hours 37 minutes
  • Year length: 687 Earth days
  • Thin atmosphere, mostly CO2
  • Evidence of ancient water activity

MARS MISSION

Curiosity Rover

Exploring Gale Crater since 2012, searching for signs of ancient microbial life.

Curiosity is a car-sized Mars rover exploring Gale crater and Mount Sharp on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission.

Perseverance Rover

Landed in 2021, collecting rock samples and testing oxygen production from the Martian atmosphere.

Perseverance, nicknamed Percy, is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Jezero crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars 2020 mission.

Ingenuity Helicopter

First powered, controlled flight on another planet, demonstrating future exploration possibilities.

Ingenuity, nicknamed Ginny, is an autonomous NASA helicopter that operated on Mars from 2021 to 2024 as part of the Mars 2020 mission.

Mars Sample Return

A future mission to bring samples back from Mars to Earth for detailed analysis.

This mission aims to collect and return Martian soil and rock samples to Earth for in-depth study.
